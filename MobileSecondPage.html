RUNNING WORLD CUP 2015

Not everyone gets the opportunity to represent their country but in this one you do. No matter your speed or distance, the aim of the game is to run as much as possible for your country to help them reach the top of the leaderboard. The more runs you do; the more km points your country scores. There are 3 knockout rounds before you can be crowned a running world cup champion.
